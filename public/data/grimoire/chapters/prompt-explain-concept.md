# 解释概念

用一句话概括一段文字，测试摘要能力。

## 提示

```
Antibiotics are a type of medication used to treat bacterial infections. They work by either killing the bacteria or preventing them from reproducing, allowing the body's immune system to fight off the infection. Antibiotics are usually taken orally in the form of pills, capsules, or liquid solutions, or sometimes administered intravenously. They are not effective against viral infections, and using them inappropriately can lead to antibiotic resistance.

Explain the above in one sentence:
```

## 预期输出

模型应将整段文字压缩为一句简洁的概括，如：

> Antibiotics are medications that treat bacterial infections by killing or inhibiting bacteria, but they are ineffective against viruses and misuse can lead to resistance.

## 要点

- 文本摘要是 LLM 最常见的应用场景之一
- 通过明确要求"一句话"来控制输出长度
- 适用于快速理解长文本的核心内容
